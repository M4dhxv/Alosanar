Today’s data centers should be designed and built with future operational performance in mind, and much more dynamic capabilities, economics and vision. This means more progressive thinking around cooling, varying electrical loads, fire life safety, energy, efficiency, and sustainability as well as changing staffing paradigms. Even the location of edge data centers is a creative exercise when repurposing former real estate use-types.

Agentic AI and AI-ready systems ontology are table stakes for data center facility management in order to handle the array of risk, performance, environmental and energy challenges on both the supply and demand sides.
